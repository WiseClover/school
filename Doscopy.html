Pavlivskyi Lyceum of Volyn Region

Welcome to our official website!

Pavlivskyi Lyceum is a modern educational institution that offers high-quality education for middle and senior school students. Our goal is to provide students with the opportunity to gain in-depth knowledge, develop essential skills for a successful career, and foster responsible, creative citizens of Ukraine.

Our priorities

  • Individual approach to each student, considering their strengths and interests.
  • Modern teaching methods using innovative technologies.
  • Active student participation in cultural and sports events that promote overall development.
  • Preparation for external independent evaluation (EIE) and university admission.

Why choose our lyceum?

  • Highly qualified teachers who use advanced teaching methodologies.
  • Diverse electives and clubs that allow students to develop their interests and talents.
  • Modern technical and material base, comfortable conditions for learning and recreation.
  • Active collaboration with parents for successful student learning.
  • Sports and cultural achievements—participation in regional and national competitions.

How to get here?

The lyceum is located in the village of Pavlivka, Volyn region. It is easy to reach from both the regional center and neighboring settlements.

Support and innovations

The lyceum constantly improves its educational programs by introducing the latest technologies and methods. We also actively collaborate with various educational institutions and organizations, helping our students develop in various fields.
